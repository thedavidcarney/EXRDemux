================================================================
After Effects Plug-in SDK
================================================================

The compiled .aex includes utility code (AEGP_SuiteHandler,
AEFX_SuiteHelper, MissingSuiteError) from Adobe's After Effects
Plug-in SDK, used per the SDK's terms. Adobe SDK headers are not
redistributed in this repository — developers obtain them from the
Adobe Developer portal.

================================================================
Acknowledgement (not a license obligation, just gratitude)
================================================================

EXRDemux is a workflow-compatible reimplementation of EXtractoR by
Brendan Bolles / fnordware (https://www.fnordware.com/ProEXR/), which
has been the standard tool for multilayer EXR work in After Effects
for almost two decades. EXRDemux contains none of EXtractoR's code,
but its parameter design (Black Point, White Point, UnMult) follows
EXtractoR's conventions deliberately to preserve user muscle memory,
and Brendan's long-running contributions to OpenEXR itself underpin
the libraries we statically link. Thanks.
